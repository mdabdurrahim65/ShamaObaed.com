About Shama Obaed

Shama Obaed is BNP Organising Secretary and candidate for Faridpur-2 constituency. She focuses on democratic rights, human welfare, and service to society.

She emphasizes that Islam teaches humanity and service, not exploitation.

Before elections, she renounced her U.S. citizenship to fully commit to Bangladesh.
